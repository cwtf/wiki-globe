How to explore Wikipedia
  1. Click anywhere on the globe or map to search Wikipedia for places near that spot. A panel of nearby articles opens on the right.
  2. Drag the Search radius slider in that panel to widen or narrow the area you're searching.
  3. The glowing circle on the map shows exactly how far out you're looking. It grows and shrinks as you move the slider.
  4. Every result with a known location gets a W pin on the map. Click a pin to open its Wikipedia article in a popup, or click an article to find its pin.
  5. Click “Read on Wikipedia” in any result to open the full article in a new tab.

ABOUT WIKI GLOBElive data · open knowledge · deep space

Wiki Globe is an interactive 3D atlas for exploring Earth, the Moon, Mars, the Sun, planets, and major moons through live movement layers, map data, and nearby Wikipedia articles.

What you can do
  • Track satellites, flights, ships, and shipping corridors.
  • Compare true-size country and continent outlines.
  • Color the world with weather, population, economy, health, climate, and conflict overlays.
  • Click places and surface markers to discover related Wikipedia articles.
  • Ask the globe agent to research, reason, highlight countries, and draw on the map.
Data sources

Built with CesiumJS, OpenStreetMap, CelesTrak, OpenSky, adsbdb, Open-Meteo, World Bank, OWID, UNDP, Wikidata, Wikipedia, NASA imagery, Solar System Scope textures, and AIS feeds from aisstream and Digitraffic.
