Wiki Globe is an interactive map for exploring satellite orbits, flight paths, shipping routes, OpenStreetMap detail, and Wikipedia articles near any selected location on Earth.

How to explore Wikipedia
  1. Click anywhere on the globe or map to search Wikipedia for places near that spot. A panel of nearby articles opens on the right.
  2. Drag the Search radius slider in that panel to widen or narrow the area you're searching.
  3. The glowing circle on the map shows exactly how far out you're looking. It grows and shrinks as you move the slider.
  4. Every result with a known location gets a W pin on the map. Click a pin to highlight its article, or click an article to find its pin.
  5. Click “Read on Wikipedia” in any result to open the full article in a new tab.
